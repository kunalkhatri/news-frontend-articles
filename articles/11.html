Indians Among 66 Evacuated From Conflict-Hit Sudan To Saudi

Saudi Arabia has announced its decision to help evacuate Indians from Sudan, in the wake of civil unrest in the country. This announcement comes just a few days after India's Minister of External Affairs S Jaishankar had urged Saudi Arabia to help organise safe passage for Indians who were to be brought back home.

The Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) had requested the Gulf nation’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs last week to help organise safe passage for Indian nationals residing in Sudan amidst a civil unrest in the country due to army intervention. The MEA on Wednesday confirmed that Saudi Arabia has agreed to their request and will be facilitating safe evacuation for the Indians from Sudan.
