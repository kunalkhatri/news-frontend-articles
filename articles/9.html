UK To Test Emergency Alert System On Millions Of Phones Today

Today, the United Kingdom is testing an emergency alert system that will send alerts to millions of phones across the country. The test will start at 11:00am and will last for one minute. This alert system, called the 'Public Warning System', was developed to give people more information during emergencies.

The UK government has created a new emergency alert system that they are testing today. The goal of this system is to give people up-to-date information quickly in order to help them remain safe during an emergency. This test involves sending a one-minute alert to millions of phones at 11 am in order to make sure that it is working properly. This alert system is created for everyone in the UK so hopefully it provides people with helpful information during emergencies.
