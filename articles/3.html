"Price For Speaking Truth": Rahul Gandhi After Vacating Delhi Bungalow

Rahul Gandhi, a senior Congress leader and former president of the Indian National Congress party, vacated his Delhi bungalow recently. He did this to stand up for democracy and urge everyone to speak the truth.

He mentioned that people who tell the truth are often harassed or silenced in India. He also said that those in power become worried when people start speaking the truth. His gesture was applauded by many people on social media as it showcased courage and strength.

The message of truth that Rahul Gandhi has put out could be summed up as: one must never shy away from speaking their truth despite any consequences that might come with it. It is important for every individual to have an opinion and stand true to their beliefs. One must take action when they feel something is wrong or not correct. Moreover, nobody should be afraid of speaking the truth in a democracy.

Rahul Gandhi is a role model for all children's at present, he has put forth an important message about democratic values: always stand up for justice and show courage in speaking the truth. His motivation can inspire every 10-year-old to never be scared of standing against wrongdoing and always speak their mind fearlessly and boldly.
