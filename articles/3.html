"Fabricated Audio Clip": Tamil Nadu Minister On MK Stalin's "Family" Row

Tamil Nadu Minister Palanivel Thiaga Rajan has talked aboutMK Stalin's family row, saying that a malicious and fabricated audio clip was being spread. He went on to explain that the clip is false news and claimed that it is being made use of to drive wedges into parties. Rajan also said that it is sad that such clips are being circulated in order to sweeten political one-upmanship and being used to stir up controversies.

The Tamil Nadu Minister had a message for 10-year-olds reading this article: Spreading fake news is wrong and can be damaging, not just for politicians but for everyone. These malicious audio clips can be used to cause conflict between people, leading to distrust and hurt. So don't believe in rumours or anything you hear from people trying to create drama - it's not true!
