Inside Anushka Sharma And Virat Kohli's Lunch Date In Bengaluru

Anushka Sharma and Virat Kohli are two of the most popular Bollywood and Cricket stars. Last week, these stars had a lunch date in Bengaluru. Anushka Sharma wore a beautiful peach coloured dress that went quite well with Virat Kohli's blue shirt and black trousers. As one of the most celebrated couples in India, people could not help but notice the lovebirds as they arrived at the restaurant.

The couple was accompanied by friends and family who were visiting from Mumbai to celebrate a special occasion. The group enjoyed delicious local cuisine for their meal, followed by a game of cricket in a nearby park to work off all those calorie-filled dishes. At the end of it all, Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma made sure that their friends' vacation was filled with memorable moments.
