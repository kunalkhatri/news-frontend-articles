"Brainless": Prasad Blasts KL Rahul's LSG After Shocking Loss Against GT

The Indian Premier League (IPL) has been full of excitement, and one of its latest matches between the Gujarat Titans and the Lucknow Supergiants was no exception. After a shocking loss, former Indian cricketer Venkatesh Prasad was furious.

Prasad thought that the players of the Lucknow Supergiants were 'brainless' for not playing more smartly and said that their performance was simply 'not acceptable'. He did not spare any player in particular, criticizing them all. He thought that the Lucknow Supergiants were sure to win, but due to what Prasad called as ‘mental affliction’, they fell to defeat at the hands of Gujarat Titans.

A recent cricket match between two teams had a shock result! Former cricketer Venkatesh Prasad thought that the unlucky team (Lucknow Supergiants) needed to play smarter. He felt they didn't use their brains well and lost to Gujarat Titans against expectations. Prasad couldn't believe it when they lost and blamed them for having mental issues which got in the way!
