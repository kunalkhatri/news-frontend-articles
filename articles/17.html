Watch: Fiery Arshdeep's Stump-Breaking Spree vs Rohit's MI Wows All

Exhilarating scenes were witnessed at the Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai as Arshdeep Singh of Punjab Kings wowed the audience with a sensational performance against Rohit Sharma's Mumbai Indians. Singh took six wickets including three times breaking the stumps to lead his team to a terrific win over MI.

A ten year old will be thrilled by tearaway pacer Arshdeep Singh's remarkable performance at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai. Delhi native, Singh was playing against a powerful side led by none other than skipper Rohit Sharma of MI. The teenager bowled with fire and vigour and made sure that the opposition batsmen were kept under wrap due to his sheer speed, accuracy and power. His outstanding performance included taking a total of six wickets which included three stumpings that would make any cricket fan proud! His excellent spell not only helped Punjab Kings secure an emphatic win over MI but also made everyone in the stadium jump with joy!
