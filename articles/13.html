Zilingo CEO Files $100 Million Defamation Case Against Mahesh Murthy

Ankiti Bose, the CEO of Zilingo, an ecommerce company, filed a criminal complaint alleging defamation against Mahesh Murthy, one of the shareholders in Zilingo. The complaint was filed after Murthy wrote a defamatory article about her and her co-founder on his social network. In the article, he accused them of insider trading and various forms of unethical practices.

Ankiti Bose is the CEO of an online shopping platform called Zilingo. Her co-founder and fellow shareholder Mahesh Murthy recently wrote a post on his social network that said that she was involved in some unethical activities - like trading with insider information and other such things. This offended Bose who then took it to court to file a criminal complaint alleging defamation against Murthy. She hopes that by doing this she can clear her name from all false accusations and restore her reputation.
