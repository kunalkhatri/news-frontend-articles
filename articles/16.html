Delta Passenger Accused Of Grabbing, Kissing Flight Attendant: Report

A flight attendant accused a Delta passenger of grabbing and forcefully trying to kiss her during a flight from Japan to the United States. According to the report, the incident took place aboard Delta Flight 28 on January 26. The passenger allegedly put his arms around the attendant's scarf, tried to forcibly kiss her, and groped her back. After being reported about the incident immediately by his co-workers, he was apprehended by US Marshals when they touched down at Los Angeles International Airport.
