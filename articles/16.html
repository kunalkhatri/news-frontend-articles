Delta Passenger Accused Of Grabbing, Kissing Flight Attendant: Report

A passenger on an airline was recently detained after behaving inappropriately with a female flight attendant. According to a report, the unidentified Delta Airlines passenger was accused of grabbing her arm and forcibly kissing her while the aircraft was still in the air.

During his flight on Delta Airlines, the unnamed passenger allegedly grabbed a flight attendant's arm and kissed her against her will. After the plane landed at Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport in Florida, law enforcement officers boarded the aircraft and detained him for his inappropriate behaviour.

The man accused of grabbing and kissing the airline attendant without permission is facing charges for breach of peace. Delta Airlines later released a statement saying that they had requested law enforcement's assistance and offered their full cooperation during the investigation into the alleged incident.
