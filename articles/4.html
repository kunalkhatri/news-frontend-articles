"Fabricated Audio Clip": Tamil Nadu Minister On MK Stalin's "Family" Row

Tamil Nadu's Minister Palanivel Thiaga Rajan, from the All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK) party, has said that a malicious and fabricated audio clip about a dispute between MK Stalin, chief of the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK), and his family has been circulated.

Minister Rajan has stated that anyone who spreads such false information or tries to disrupt the peace in Tamil Nadu will face strict legal action. He added that this act is being done by those in favour of DMK with an intention of defaming the AIADMK.

Mr Stalin had previously shared his family photo on social media which led to an anonymous audio clip being shared intended to create differences among members in his immediate family.

The Minister emphasised on unity across both the parties and hoped that instead of creating false rumour and statements, both AIADMK and DMK should work together for betterment of Tamil Nadu.
