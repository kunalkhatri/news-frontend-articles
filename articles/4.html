"Fabricated Audio Clip": Tamil Nadu Minister On MK Stalin's "Family" Row

State Minister Palanivel Thiaga Rajan of Tamil Nadu has called an audio clip being shared on social media as malicious and fabricated. This audio clip is related to the ongoing family dispute involving Chief minister of the State and his elder brother MK Stalin.

Palanivel Thiaga was addressing media persons in Erode district of the state when he described this malicious and fabricated audio clip as an additional testimony to their withstanding family feud. He further clarified that this does not help in any way to solve the dispute which has been continuing for a long time.
