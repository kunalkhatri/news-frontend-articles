A 14-year-old girl in Uttar Dinajpur, West Bengal was brutally raped and killed recently. Leaders of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) have blamed Trinamool Congress (TC) for attempting to cover up the evidence by diluting it, which is why they are demanding justice for the victim and her family. The BJP leader also alleged that local Trinamool Congress or TC workers were involved in the crime and called for an investigation on their involvement.

The BJP has asked TMC Ministers to address this issue, uphold basic human rights, ensure safety and security of women in Bengal and hold those responsible accountable for this heinous crime. Meanwhile, the Uttar Dinajpur administration has ordered a probe on what happened as well as arresting all accused persons who played a part in carrying out this awful incident.

The transgender community in West Bengal has raised its voice against this incident too and various organisations have held protests across parts of West Bengal to demand justice for not just the victim but to condemn violence against women everywhere. This outrage did not stop there, people from all walks of life have joined in this nationwide campaign against such terrible brutality that should never be tolerated.
