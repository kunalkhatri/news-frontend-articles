"Price For Speaking Truth": Rahul Gandhi After Vacating Delhi Bungalow

Rahul Gandhi, a great leader of India, has been asked to vacate the bungalow which he used to stay in Delhi. It has happened due to him speaking the truth about government and several other issues. There is something special about this giant leader. Despite the pressure of the government on him he never stops speaking his heart and mind out.

This incident shows the amount of courage and respect Rahul Gandhi has got in his heart. He never shies away from telling the truth no matter what it takes in terms of penalties and pressure from the government. Everyone must admire such courage and respect for truth. This also motivates many others to speak up their minds without any fear.

In this matter, Rahul Gandhi was asked to vacate a bungalow he was assigned as a Member of Parliament because he said some true things that are unfavorable for some people in power. Even after all this pressure, he is firm on his character and stands by what is right by raising voice against injustice even if there is a penalty associated with it.
