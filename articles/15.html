IPL 2023: KL Rahul Registers Unwanted Batting Record In Loss Against GT

KL Rahul, the skipper of Lucknow Super Giants, made an unwanted batting record in the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2023 match against Gujarat Titans. In this match, Lucknow Super Giants batsmen played very badly and KL Rahul scored only two runs in seven balls. This batting performance made him the first captain in the history of IPL to score two or fewer runs in his team’s total score of seven or more wickets. The match was won by Gujarat Titans quite comfortably as they scored a massive 175 runs with nine wickets still alive.

KL Rahul had a very disappointing day as captain of the Lucknow Super Giants. He started well but couldn't manage to make it count and ended up registering an unwanted record by scoring only two runs off seven balls. This poor batting display resulted in his team losing the game as Gujarat Titans scored a very impressive 175 runs with nine wickets remaining. KL Rahul’s performance was not up to mark with this much needed game which resulted in his team losing the match very badly.
