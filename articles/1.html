Fugitive Preacher Amritpal Singh Surrenders Before Punjab Cops: Sources

Fugitive preacher Amritpal Singh has given himself up to the police in the state of Punjab. He has been taken into custody and currently being questioned by the police.

Amritpal Singh had been wanted for several weeks after a video recording of him making inflammatory speeches surfaced. His comments were particularly inflammatory against the Sikh priesthood and were linked to a religious group he was affiliated with. After his video sparked public outrage, police started searching for Amritpal.

Amritpal finally surrendered at a Police Station in Moga (Punjab). As per reports, he arrived at the station with his father and other family members. He will be questioned by Punjab Police, who are taking necessary steps before filing a chargesheet against him.
