Fugitive Preacher Amritpal Singh Surrenders In Punjab, Arrested

A religious preacher named Amritpal Singh, who had gone missing recently, has surrendered himself to the police in Moga in Punjab. Sources say he was taken into custody by the police from there.

Amritpal Singh was a religious preacher from Rode town in Moga district of Punjab and had gone missing just a few days ago. The police looked high and low for Amritpal and then tracked him down to Moga. After his surrender, he was detained by the police for further investigation as to why he went missing.

Amritpal Singh's disappearance had sparked worry among his family and friends. His wife appealed to the Deputy Chief Minister of Punjab to help locate her husband as she did not know where he had gone to or why he had left the house suddenly.

After his surrender, Amritpal Singh has now been detained by the police until they investigate why he disappeared in the first place.
