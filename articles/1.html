Fugitive Preacher Amritpal Singh Surrenders Before Punjab Cops: Sources

Amritpal Singh, an infamous preacher, has surrendered himself before the Punjab police in Moga. He had been on the run since being accused of instigating a gathering of around 200 people in November at the Sikh shrine of Sri Nankana Sahib against Pakistani authorities. Sources have confirmed that he has been detained by the police after his surrender. His act was criticised harshly by both Indian and Pakistani governments.

Amritpal Singh, an infamous religious figure, has been arrested after giving himself up to police in Moga, India.
