"All About Mindgame": Law Minister Over Judges' Appointment System Row

The Collegium System is a very important part of Indian law. It is the process of top judges in the country appointing other judges to higher courts. Union Minister of State for Home Affairs Kiren Rijiju recently said he couldn't comment on this issue as it was more about mind games occurring between the government and judiciary.

Rijiju was commenting on the Centre's decision to return a file recommending the elevation of a Madras High Court judge to the Supreme Court for reconsideration. The Collegium processing this recommendation includes India's Chief Justice and other senior-most judges at the Supreme Court.

Rijiju said opinions on such matters should be formed with deeper understanding and knowledge about its repercussions, and that commenting without knowledge would make no sense. He added that when people will get an in depth knowledge about this matter, only then can proper conclusions be drawn.
