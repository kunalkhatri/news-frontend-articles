"All About Mindgame": Law Minister Over Judges' Appointment System Row

The Department of Law and the Supreme Court of India are having a disagreement, called the Collegium Issue. Union Minister Kiren Rijiju, who is from the Government of India, has commented on the situation. He said it is all a ‘mindgame’ and he refuses to provide his opinion on it.

Rijiju compared the situation to a 'mindgame' and mentioned he would not comment any further. The Collegium Issue is between the Department of Law and Supreme Court. The disagreement has been ongoing since December 2018 without resolution. It surrounds the issue of appointing judges to high courts in India. Rijiju said any comments made might be used against him in court, so he preferred not to comment on this case.
